# MP Marketing - Quick Start Guide

## First-Time Setup

### 1. Create Your Account
1. Open MP Marketing app
2. Tap "Create Account" on login screen
3. Enter:
   - Full Name
   - Email address
   - Password (min 6 characters)
   - Confirm password
4. Tap "Create Account"
5. You'll be automatically signed in

### 2. Complete Your Profile
1. Tap "Profile" tab
2. Tap edit button (pencil icon)
3. Add:
   - Profile photo
   - Phone number
   - Location (city/state)
   - Bio/description
4. Save changes

## Buying Items

### Search for Products
1. Open app to "Home" tab
2. Use search bar to find items:
   - "iPhone 14"
   - "Used bicycle"
   - "Furniture"
3. Tap search icon
4. Browse results

### Browse by Category
1. Tap "Categories" tab
2. See all 10 categories:
   - Electronics, Vehicles, Property, Fashion
   - Home & Garden, Sports, Jobs, Services, Pets, Books
3. Tap category to view products
4. Use search within category to narrow down

### View Product Details
1. Tap any product card
2. View:
   - All product photos
   - Full description
   - Price and negotiability
   - Product condition
   - Seller information
   - Review/rating
3. Tap "Contact Seller" to chat
4. Or tap heart to save to favorites

### Save Favorite Items
1. On product card, tap heart icon (top right)
2. Heart turns red when saved
3. View saved items in Profile → Saved Items
4. Tap item again to remove from favorites

### Contact Seller
1. Open product details
2. Tap "Contact Seller" button
3. Chat window opens
4. Type your message
5. Tap send (paper plane icon)
6. Seller will respond in real-time

### View Your Conversations
1. Tap "Messages" tab
2. See all active conversations
3. Latest message preview shown
4. Tap conversation to open full chat
5. View product being discussed

## Selling Items

### Create Your First Listing
1. Tap "Sell" tab (plus icon)
2. Enter product details:
   - **Title**: "Describe your item briefly"
   - **Category**: Select from dropdown
   - **Price**: Enter amount in USD
   - **Description**: Full product details
   - **Condition**: New/Like New/Good/Fair/Poor
   - **Location**: City and state
   - **Price Negotiable**: Toggle if price flexible

### Add Photos
1. Tap "Add Photos" area
2. Select up to 10 photos
3. Reorder photos by dragging
4. First photo is main image

### Post Your Listing
1. Review all information
2. Tap "Post Listing"
3. Listing appears on marketplace
4. You'll get notifications when people contact you

### Manage Your Listings
1. Tap "Profile" tab
2. See "My Listings" section
3. Each listing shows:
   - View count
   - Current status
   - Posted date
4. Tap listing to:
   - View details
   - Edit information
   - Mark as sold
   - Archive listing

### Respond to Buyers
1. Tap "Messages" tab
2. Open conversation
3. Type reply
4. Tap send button
5. Continue conversation

## Safety & Trust

### Verify Seller
- Look for checkmark badge (verified seller)
- Read seller ratings
- Check review comments
- Message before committing

### Report Issues
1. Open product or conversation
2. Tap flag icon (report)
3. Select reason
4. Provide description
5. Submit report
6. Our team reviews reports

### Protect Yourself
- Don't share personal info early
- Meet in public places
- Bring someone with you
- Verify items before payment
- Use secure payment methods

## Common Questions

**Q: Is my payment secure?**
A: We use Supabase authentication. Don't share passwords or payment info in chat.

**Q: How quickly do sellers respond?**
A: Most sellers respond within a few hours during business hours.

**Q: Can I edit my listing after posting?**
A: Yes, tap your listing in Profile and select Edit.

**Q: What if I can't reach the seller?**
A: Send message through app. If no response after 24 hours, move on to another listing.

**Q: How do I know if someone is legit?**
A: Check their profile rating, reviews, and how long they've been a member.

**Q: Can I negotiate prices?**
A: Yes! Items marked "negotiable" allow price discussions via chat.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support through Settings menu.
